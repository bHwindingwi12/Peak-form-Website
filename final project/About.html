Our Mission

The Player's Blueprint

Player's Blue Print aims to help student athletes with reliable information on nutrition, training guidance, and resources to improve their physical performance.

What we are hoping to accomplish is to help students train more effectively and lower the risk of injuries.

Who This Is For

Our primary focus is college student athletes, but the site is accessible to all genders, with an age range from about 14-25.
